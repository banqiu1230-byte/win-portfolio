# TEMU After-sales Motion

## Style Prompt

Warm-white editorial product showcase. Real interface screens remain fully visible inside refined rounded device cards. Motion is calm, precise, and system-oriented: vertical interface streams establish scale, then converge into a clear three-step after-sales journey. TEMU orange is the only accent.

## Colors

- Canvas: `#F2F1EE`
- Surface: `#FFFFFF`
- Primary text: `#1E1D1B`
- Secondary text: `#77736D`
- Accent: `#FF6A00`

## Typography

- `SF Pro Display`, `PingFang SC`, `Helvetica Neue`, sans-serif

## Motion

- Use opacity, translate, and scale only.
- Use `power3.inOut` and `power4.out`; no bounce or elastic easing.
- Screens move as a coordinated system, not as independent decorative objects.

## What NOT to Do

- Do not crop interface content.
- Do not add extra colors, gradients, or glass effects.
- Do not use large marketing copy over the UI.
- Do not use fast cuts or playful bounce.
